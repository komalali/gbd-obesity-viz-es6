Obesity is on the rise globally.

Between 1980 and 2016, the global prevalence of obesity (defined as the percentage of the population with a BMI > 30) rose from 5.2% to 14.0%, a relative increase of 269%!

High BMI is associated with a huge variety of health problems, ranging from heart disease to diabetes to various forms of cancer.
