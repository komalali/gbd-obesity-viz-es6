Obesity is on the rise globally.

Between 1990 and 2013, the global prevalence of obesity (defined as the percentage of the population with a BMI > 30) rose from 9.3% to 12.0%, a relative increase of 29%.

High BMI is associated with a huge variety of health problems, ranging from heart disease to diabetes to various forms of cancer.
